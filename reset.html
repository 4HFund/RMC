A guided pause

The 5-Minute
Calm Reset

A simple guided pause for the mom who is holding everything together but needs a moment to come back to herself.

This is not another thing to accomplish. It is a small space to breathe, soften, and reset.

Notice

First, notice where you are.

What feels heaviest right now?
My mind will not stop racing.
My body feels tense.
I feel emotionally drained.
I feel behind on everything.
I feel like I might snap.
I just need a moment.
That makes sense. You do not have to fix everything right now. Just start here.
Breathe

Let your body know
you are here.

Place one hand on your chest and one hand on your belly. We will breathe together three times.

Ready
Cycle 1 of 3
You are allowed to slow down.
Ground

Come back to
right now.

Look around your physical space. Let your senses remind you that this moment is here.
I am choosing 1 small thing for right now:
Release

Set one thing down.

What is one thing you are carrying right now that you can set down for the next five minutes?

You do not have to release everything. Just one thing. Just for now.

Reconnect

Choose what your body
needs to hear.

Pick one sentence to carry with you today.
I am safe in this moment.
I do not have to carry everything alone.
I can slow down without falling behind.
My needs matter too.
I am allowed to breathe.
I can take the next step, not every step.
Complete

You made space
to breathe.

That moment mattered. You paused. You noticed. You released a little pressure. You came back to yourself.
